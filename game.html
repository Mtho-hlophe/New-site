Rules of the game

The game is made out of the hunting logic, the rabbit is being hunted by three dogs. This game is a two player game, one player must be the rabbit, and the other player be the dogs!

Movement rules

The rabbit is the first to move!
Players can move in any direction but one block at a time, and a player cannot jump boxes.
The rabbit can slip through the dogs by moving diagonally The dogs can only catch the Rabbit by trapping it into a corner whereby every movement the rabbit has is straight forward to the dog's block!
